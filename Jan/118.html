BROWSERS

        - majority of people used in 2014.
        - pre-installed on mirosoft windows.
        - From 2015 Microsoft Edge replaced IE (Windows 10 OS)

Google Chrome:
        - 60% users in 2016.
        - Freeware browser developed by Google.
        - First release in 2008.
        - Greater Security.
        - later ported to Linux, MacOS, IOS, & Android.

Firefox:
        - Free & open-source developed by Mozilla.
