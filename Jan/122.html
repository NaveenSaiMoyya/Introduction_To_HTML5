Semantic Tags

=> makes a webpage accessible to as many people as possible.
=> maso important aspect in web development is Design.

Divisions:   <div>
           - were a way to group related context together.
           - has special classes/ids like headers, footer, section.
           - replaced with semantic tags.


header:     <header>
       - group of introductory or navigational links/aids.
       - block level tag.


nav:         <nav>
       - section of page that links to other pages or to parts within the page.
       - often found in header tag.


footer:      <footer>
       - section that contains copyright data, related documents, & social media links.
       - typically(generally) at bottom of the page.


figure:      <figure>
       - more semantic than image tag.
       - can include caption, multiple multi-media resourses.
