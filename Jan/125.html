MULTI-MEDIA

=> before HTML5, there was no consistent use of multimedia. plug-ins were used.
=> plug-ins like Quicktime, Flash must be downloaded.

<video>:
            - src attribute or embedded <source>
            - height, weight, autoplay, loop, controls

<audio>:
            - src attribute 
            - autoplay, loop, controls, buffered, muted, volume

Setting Clips:
      - to play clips by adding to src attribute.
      .ext#t=5,25
      .ext#t=,39
      .ext#t=,1:38:45
      .ext#t=42

Best Practices:
>providing links to plug-ins (outdated)
>include closed captions (CC)
>Multimedia should enhance the content, but not be a distraction.
